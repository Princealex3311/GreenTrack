About GreenTrack

Project Overview

GreenTrack is a comprehensive waste management system designed to address the pressing issues of waste accumulation and inadequate disposal in Kenyan urban areas. By leveraging technology, we aim to streamline the entire waste management lifecycle, from resident reporting to optimized collection and sustainable recycling.

Our purpose is to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable urban environment for all, while also creating economic opportunities in the recycling sector.

Justification & Impact

  • Environmental Impact: Reduce pollution, minimize landfill waste, and promote a circular economy through efficient recycling.
  • Public Health Risks: Mitigate health hazards associated with poor sanitation and improper waste disposal, leading to healthier communities.
  • Economic Opportunity: Create jobs in waste collection, sorting, and recycling, contributing to local economic growth.
  • Technological Innovation: Introduce modern solutions like GIS for route optimization and real-time data analytics.
  • SDG Alignment: Contribute directly to several UN Sustainable Development Goals, including SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities) and SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production).
